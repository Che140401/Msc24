Why MSC?

Mindfulness is the art of being in the moment, in the here and now. To be mindful is to be fully aware of the present experience. By maintaining a moment-by-moment awareness of our thoughts, feelings, bodily sensations, and surrounding environment, through a gentle, nurturing lens we canmake Mindfulness a way of our life.
